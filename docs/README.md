# ZeroDayClose

> **AI-native Finance Operation System with Audit-Ready Automation**

**ZeroDayClose** is an AI-powered accounting reconciliation platform that enables mid-market companies to close their books in **1 day instead of 10 days** through intelligent automation powered by context graph technology.

Unlike traditional accounting software that relies on rigid rules or basic pattern matching, ZeroDayClose builds a **living knowledge graph** that learns each firm's unique accounting DNA â€” capturing vendor relationships, approval patterns, seasonal trends, and policy exceptions.

## The Problem

<details>
<summary><strong>Current Pain Points</strong></summary>

| Pain Point | Impact |
|------------|--------|
| **Slow Close Cycles** | 53% of mid-market companies take 7-10 days to close books |
| **Manual Reconciliation** | Accountants spend 60% of their time on data entry and matching |
| **Excel Dependency** | Despite $M invested in ERPs, teams still export to Excel for reconciliation |
| **Talent Shortage** | 75% of accountants retiring by 2030, 30% drop in new CPAs |
| **Missing Documentation** | Receipts scattered across email/Slack, impossible to find |
| **Context Loss** | AI tools don't understand firm-specific patterns and policies |

</details>
